Shipping timeline

Orders ship from United States, Pennsylvania. After an order is placed and payment is verified, please allow normal processing time before shipment is prepared.

Estimated delivery time is typically 1 to 2 business weeks after shipment, depending on destination, carrier movement, holidays, weather, and other delays outside our control.

Order status and tracking

  • Payment verification: Credit card orders begin processing after payment approval. Crypto orders are manually verified after payment is received.
  • Status updates: Your customer-facing order status updates inside My Account as the order moves from awaiting payment to processing, packed, shipped, and completed.
  • Tracking: Tracking appears in your order history once it is added after shipment.
  • Research-use confirmation: Every order requires research-use-only confirmation before checkout can be completed.

Payment options

Credit card checkout supports Visa and Mastercard through the secure payment processor when card payments are configured. Card numbers are not stored on this website.

Crypto payments receive an automatic 5% discount at checkout. For BTC, SOL, or USDC, send payment to the wallet shown at checkout. Type your order number in the payment note, memo, or reference field if your wallet allows it. If not, send the transaction hash with your order number to orders@researchpeps.us.

Crypto verification

Crypto orders are saved as awaiting payment until the payment is manually verified. After verification, the admin dashboard updates the order status shown to the customer.
